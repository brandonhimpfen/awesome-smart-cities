## Urban Data Platforms

Platforms for collecting, analyzing, and visualizing city-level data.

- [ArcGIS Urban](https://www.esri.com/en-us/arcgis/products/arcgis-urban/overview) — Urban planning and geospatial data platform.
- [CKAN](https://ckan.org/) — Open-source data management system for public datasets.
- [OpenDataSoft](https://www.opendatasoft.com/) — Data portal platform for publishing and sharing city data.
- [Socrata](https://www.tylertech.com/products/socrata) — Open data and performance management platform for governments.

## Mobility & Transportation

Technologies and platforms for managing traffic, public transport, and mobility systems.

- [INRIX](https://inrix.com/) — Traffic data and mobility analytics platform.
- [Moovit](https://moovit.com/) — Public transit planning and real-time navigation app.
- [Siemens Mobility](https://www.siemens.com/mobility) — Smart mobility and transportation infrastructure solutions.
- [Transit App](https://transitapp.com/) — Real-time public transit tracking and navigation platform.

## Energy & Utilities

Smart grid technologies and systems for managing energy, water, and utilities.

- [Enel X](https://www.enelx.com/) — Smart energy management and demand response solutions.
- [Itron](https://www.itron.com/) — Smart metering and utility management technologies.
- [Schneider Electric EcoStruxure](https://www.se.com/ww/en/work/campaign/innovation/overview.jsp) — IoT-enabled energy and automation platform.

## Public Safety & Infrastructure

Systems for monitoring, maintaining, and improving urban infrastructure and safety.

- [Genetec](https://www.genetec.com/) — Unified security and surveillance platform.
- [ShotSpotter](https://www.soundthinking.com/) — Gunshot detection and public safety analytics system.
- [Trimble](https://www.trimble.com/) — Infrastructure and construction technology solutions.

## IoT & Connectivity

Platforms and technologies enabling connected devices and urban sensing networks.

- [Cisco Smart+Connected Communities](https://www.cisco.com/c/en/us/solutions/industries/smart-connected-communities.html) — IoT and networking solutions for smart cities.
- [Libelium](https://www.libelium.com/) — IoT hardware and sensors for smart city deployments.
- [ThingsBoard](https://thingsboard.io/) — Open-source IoT platform for device management and data visualization.

## Governance & Civic Tech

Tools and platforms supporting digital governance, citizen engagement, and public services.

- [Decidim](https://decidim.org/) — Open-source platform for participatory democracy.
- [OpenGov](https://opengov.com/) — Cloud software for budgeting, performance, and civic engagement.
- [SeeClickFix](https://seeclickfix.com/) — Platform for reporting and resolving local issues.

## Sustainability & Environment

Technologies focused on environmental monitoring, climate resilience, and sustainable urban systems.

- [BreezoMeter](https://www.breezometer.com/) — Air quality data and environmental intelligence platform.
- [Clarity Movement](https://www.clarity.io/) — Air pollution monitoring and analytics.
- [UrbanFootprint](https://urbanfootprint.com/) — Urban planning platform for sustainability and resilience analysis.
